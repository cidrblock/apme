## Third-party strategy plugin (M025)

Third-party strategy plugins are deprecated; only ansible.builtin strategies are supported (2.23)

**Removal version**: 2.23
**Fix tier**: 3
**Audience**: content

### Detection

Detect strategy: not in ansible.builtin.*

Detects `strategy:` on plays that is not a built-in strategy (`linear`, `free`,
`debug`, `host_pinned`) or `ansible.builtin.*`. Play-level attributes require
full playbook scanning; examples cannot be tested in the doc integration harness.

### Remediation

Informational warning only
